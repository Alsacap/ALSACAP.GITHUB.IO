About Us

Our mission is to look for opportunities around the United States and take position in them before they become popular in the market. From public to private companies, currencies to commodities, and startups to large business, ALSACAP will be there before the crowd.

This goal will be achieved by investing our resources in innovative research to find in advance which direction the market and people's sentiment move to next. Our research resources range from global indices analysis to personal connections with market makers

We use an aggressive investment approach, yet we are able to reduce the down risk significantly by diversifying our portfolio between different types of securities and significantly hedging the high risk - high reward securities. In addition, we place our capital in the highest yield forms of securities and constantly monitor factors such as volatility and economical data to alert us in case of an incoming shift in the market direction.

Investment Categories

Public Companies

Identifying and focusing on quality stocks that will generate high yield of returns to the firm as value and growth are in focus. Our plan targets a diversified portfolio in Technology, Consumer Cyclical, Green Energy, and Fintech companies which will generate the highest returns over the upcoming years.

Indices

Because they provide an overall exposure to specific markets, Indices are a great fit for investment growth when betting into economies. From commodities like gold and oil to currencies like the dollar, Indices provide the exposure needed to the firm's portfolio to these economies.

Corporate Bonds

Corporate Bonds provide a double benefit to our portfolio, the annual interest collected from the issuer, while still being able to sell it for a premimum in secondary markets. It's also considered one of the safe havens form of securities as long as the business stay healthy and maintain a low risk level.

Small Business Lending

Providing short term lending to small businesses at the lowest interest rate, or in exchange for shares in the business. This helps the small business achieve their short-term goals while not having to adjust their budget due to high interest rates and allow better usage of the funds awarded.

Commercial Real Estate

We take minority stakes in a variety of commercial real estate projects under development around the United States. Strategically allowing limited exposure to the overall economy conditions while benefitting from the future capital growth that derives from the gain in the estate value.

Residential Real Estate

Taking stake in healthy residential properties, in thriving communities within the United States. Homeowners will benefit from the proceeds received while maintaining majority ownership of their home. As the housing market value grows over time, our stake in the housing market grow with it as well.

StartUps

Investors worldwide pour hundreds of millions of dollars annually to Silicon Valley attempting to find the next big unicorn. It takes an in-depth research and analysis of a start up's delivered product and the future consumer preference to pick the right one to take a position in.

CDs

Investing in CDs delivers assurance to the portfolio as a short-term low risk investment due to the investment accounts being Federally Insured. CDs have very low fees which allows more predictable returns.
